## 1. Overview

PayMint is a cloud POS and business management system for restaurants, cafes, bakeries, and retail businesses.

What you can do:

- Process sales quickly at the POS
- Manage products, categories, add-ons, and recipes
- Track shifts, cash movement, refunds, and discounts
- Add staff and control permissions
- View reports, receipts, and activity logs
- Manage multiple locations from one owner portal

The interface is available in English and Arabic.

## 2. First-Time Setup

Use the onboarding flow when you create a brand new location.

Steps:

1. Create your PayMint account.
2. Verify your email address.
3. Complete the onboarding wizard.
4. Enter your business name, address, currency, and location details.
5. Create the location ID and password used by the POS app.
6. Add your first products.
7. Install the POS app on the store device and sign in.

If you already have a brand and just want to add another branch, use the owner portal instead of repeating onboarding.

## 3. Daily Sales Workflow

### Start a shift

1. Sign in to the POS app.
2. If no shift is active, start a new one.
3. Count the opening cash in the drawer.
4. Enter the opening amount.
5. Confirm to begin the shift.

### Process a sale

1. Choose the product or category.
2. Add items to the cart.
3. Review quantities, notes, and add-ons.
4. Tap checkout.
5. Select the payment method.
6. Complete the sale and print or send the receipt.

### Split payments

PayMint supports split payments when a customer uses more than one payment method. You can divide the order by amount or by items, depending on the checkout flow enabled for the location.

### Hold and resume orders

You can hold an order for later and resume it from the held orders list. This is useful for tables, delivery follow-ups, or delayed pickup orders.

### Refunds and voids

1. Open the order in the Orders screen.
2. Choose refund or void.
3. Enter the reason.
4. Confirm the action.

Refund permissions depend on the role assigned to the employee.

## 4. Products, Categories, and Add-ons

### Add products

Go to Products and create or edit an item.

Recommended fields:

- Product name
- Price
- Category
- Image
- Tax settings
- Stock tracking
- Recipe or preparation details

### Categories

Use categories to keep the POS menu organized. Good category names are:

- Coffee
- Bakery
- Meals
- Drinks
- Retail Items

### Add-ons and modifiers

Use add-ons for choices such as:

- Size
- Milk type
- Toppings
- Extras

Attach the modifier group to the products that should display those options.

## 5. Recipes and Stock Tracking

PayMint supports stock tracking and recipe operations for items that need ingredients or production steps.

Use this when you want to:

- Track raw materials
- Decrease stock automatically when items are sold
- Set low-stock alerts
- Manage prep or recipe items

Best practice:

- Keep product names consistent
- Use a separate category for prep items if needed
- Review low-stock alerts daily

## 6. Staff and Permissions

Add staff from the dashboard and assign only the access they need.

Common permissions:

- Process sales
- Apply discounts
- Process refunds
- Manage products
- View reports
- Manage settings
- View activity logs

Security rules:

- Never share your password.
- Never share a staff PIN outside the team.
- Remove access immediately when an employee leaves.

## 7. Reports and Activity Logs

Use reports to review:

- Sales totals
- Payment breakdowns
- Refunds
- Discounts
- Staff performance
- Shift summaries
- Item performance

Use the activity log to see who changed products, settings, employees, or cash records.

## 8. Billing and Trial

Pricing:

- First location: 20 USD/month or 210 USD/year
- Additional locations: 17 USD/month or 180 USD/year
- New accounts include a 14-day free trial

Billing tips:

- Update your payment card before it expires.
- Download invoices from the owner portal.
- You can change between monthly and yearly billing.
- Cancel anytime from billing settings.

## 10. Offline Mode and Sync

If the internet connection drops, PayMint can continue taking basic sales.

What happens offline:

- Orders are saved locally
- The app syncs once the connection returns
- Pending items continue to upload in the background

Best practice:

- Keep the device powered and open until sync finishes
- Check the network before the next shift starts
- Review the sync status if items stay pending

## 11. Troubleshooting

### Connection failed

- Check Wi-Fi or network access
- Reopen the app
- Try another device or browser if you are on the web dashboard
- Contact support with the exact error message

### Printer not working

- Confirm the printer is powered on
- Check pairing or network connection
- Print a test receipt
- Reconnect the printer if needed

### Shift already active

- Another employee may already have an open shift
- Ask them to close it, or have an admin review the shift status

### Card payment failed

- Recheck the terminal connection
- Try the payment again
- Use another card or cash if needed

### Session expired

- Sign in again
- Use a trusted device
- Reset your password if you suspect unauthorized access
